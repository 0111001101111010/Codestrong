CODESTRONG is an international event that brings together the people who use, develop, design, and support the Appcelerator Titanium platform. CODESTRONG will be held September 18-20, 2011, at the InterContinental Hotel in San Francisco, California.

The conference features dozens of curated sessions and panels from some of the most influential people and brightest minds within the Titanium community and beyond, as well as countless opportunities for networking, sprints, informal conversations, and more.
