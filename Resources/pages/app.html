About this application

The CODESTRONG mobile application lets you view session schedules, descriptions, maps, and presenter bios from the palm of your hand, as well as live updates from the official Appcelerator Twitter feed. The CODESTRONG mobile application for iOS and Android was developed by the Appcelerator Developer Relations team using the Appcelerator Titanium mobile framework.

It was built based off of work done by the development team at Palantir.net which consisted of:

  • Larry Garfield (Lead)
  • Pat Teglia (Developer)
  • Jen Simmons (Designer)
